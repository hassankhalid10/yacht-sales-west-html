About Us

Setting the Standard in Yachting Since 1994

Founded on Granville Island in 1994, Yacht Sales West has grown from a passionate, two-person operation into a trusted name in the global and Pacific Northwest yachting communities. For over three decades, we have been dedicated to matching boaters with the perfect vessel and guiding our clients through every step of their nautical journey.

Built on a foundation of integrity, teamwork, and a lifelong passion for the water, we pride ourselves on delivering a personalized, stress-free experience that keeps your best interests at heart.
