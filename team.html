Inspiring Confidence

Founded in an empty Granville Island office in 1994 with just two old desks and a profound passion for sailing, Yacht Sales West has grown into a trusted name in the global yachting community. Today, we are a professional brokerage team built on a foundation of integrity, teamwork, and an unwavering commitment to our clients’ best interests.

We are here to help you pursue whatever makes your heart sing.
