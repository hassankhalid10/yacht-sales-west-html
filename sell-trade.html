Yacht Brokerage & Trade-Ins

Selling Your Yacht With Confidence

Since 1994, Yacht Sales West has built a reputation for honesty, integrity, and deep market insight. We don’t just go with the flow; we find dedicated, innovative ways to represent your interests fully and completely.

When you list with us, our professional team meets you at your vessel to provide a fair market value assessment. From there, we maximize your yacht’s exposure through targeted marketing and advertising programs across premier print and digital channels.

With two high-visibility locations—Granville Island in Vancouver and Port Sidney Marina in Sidney—your boat gets the attention of two thriving markets. We even offer the unique benefit of in-house moorage options at either location to help facilitate showings.
